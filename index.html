About Us

Enersol Biopower Pvt Ltd
Pioneering Sustainable Bioenergy Solutions

At Enersol Biopower Pvt Ltd, we lead the way in bioenergy innovation, offering cutting-edge solutions that redefine how energy is produced and consumed. As a trusted designer, manufacturer, supplier & exporter, we specialize in biomass gasifiers, biomass torrefaction plants, smokeless biomass stoves, biogas gensets etc.

Our Legecy of Innovation

We introduced India’s first portable biomass gasifier, setting a new benchmark in clean and efficient energy. Our solutions power rural electrification, replace diesel generators for telecom towers, and provide eco-friendly energy alternatives for agro-industries.

Beyond gasification, our expertise extends to solar-powered smokeless biomass stoves, thoughtfully designed to address the frequent power outages in villages, especially during evening hours when women and children need electricity the most. Our biomass stoves come equipped with a solar panel and battery, providing an alternative power source for the household. Women can cook smokeless meals on the stove while children study under its light, promoting health, convenience, and sustainability.

Renewable Energy Sources

Renewable energy is collected from resources that are naturally replenished on a human timescale. It plays a crucial role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and the use of fossil fuels, which are major contributors to global carbon dioxide emissions.

Major Types of Renewable Energy Sources

  • Solar Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydropower Energy
  • Biomass Energy

Biomass - Renewable Energy from Plants and Animals

Biomass energy is renewable energy derived from biological sources such as plants and animal residues. Humans have used biomass for thousands of years, from burning wood for cooking and warmth to utilizing agricultural residues, forestry waste, and organic components of municipal and industrial waste.

While wood remains a major source, modern biomass energy leverages a wide range of resources, making it an adaptable and sustainable energy option.

Our History

From Cement-Based to Portable Soundproof: The Evolution of Biomass Gasifiers


Beginnings with Cement-Based Models

Mr. Dahiya’s first step into biomass gasification began with the creation of a cement-based underground gasifier, marking the start of clean, alternative energy solutions for rural India.

Advancement to Stainless Steel

Transitioning to a standalone stainless steel gasifier, Mr. Dahiya enhanced the system’s durability and efficiency, setting a new standard in the biomass energy sector.

Innovation in Portability

Recognizing the need for mobility, Mr. Dahiya developed the portable stainless steel gasifier, making it ideal for remote and rural applications, and further enhancing accessibility and usability.

Soundproofing for Residential Use

The introduction of a soundproof portable gasifier addressed noise concerns, making the technology suitable for residential areas and enhancing user comfort.

Dual Purpose for Rural Empowerment

Expanding its utility, the gasifier was adapted for dual purposes: supporting both agricultural activities during the day and providing lighting at night, profoundly impacting rural livelihoods.
